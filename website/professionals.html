Karen Horneyed
Dr. Karen Horneyed MD
Dr. Horneyed specializes in relationship counseling, though her sessions often take a dramatic turn. Quick to suggest that your partner is “just like your mother,” she’s not afraid to stir the pot. Some clients say she’s overly blunt, while others appreciate her signature therapy style: “Tough Love, but Mostly Tough.”
Erik Sinson
Dr. Erik Sinson PhD
A master in developmental psychology, Dr. Sinson loves discussing all eight of his famous psychosocial stages, but somehow the conversation always circles back to guilt and shame. If you’ve ever felt bad about that one thing you did in middle school, he’ll assure you it was indeed a defining moment. But don't worry — according to him, you're probably only in a prolonged "identity crisis" that might last until retirement.
